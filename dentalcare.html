Our Services
Crowns & Bridges

A dental crown is a type of dental restoration that completely covers or caps a damaged tooth or a dental implant. It is usually made of porcelain, ceramic, or metal alloys, and is cemented onto the tooth or implant to improve its strength, shape, size, and appearance.

Dental Extractions

Tooth extraction is the removal of a tooth from its socket in the bone. It is usually performed as a last resort when a tooth is damaged or decayed beyond repair or if it is causing pain or infection. Tooth extraction may also be necessary.

Dental Implants

Dental implant is a prosthetic device that is surgically placed into the jawbone to replace missing teeth. It is typically made of biocompatible materials, such as titanium or zirconia, that fuse with the bone over time and provide a stable foundation for a replacement tooth or a bridge.

Dentures

Dentures, which are also called “false teeth,” are a common dental solution for people who have lost a lot of teeth because of an accident, illness, or something else. we know how missing teeth can affect a person’s quality of life. That’s why we offer a wide range of denture treatment options to help restore your smile.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is a branch of dentistry that deals with the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of dental and facial irregularities. It primarily focuses on the correction of malocclusions, which are misalignments of teeth and jaws.Orthodontic treatment involves the use of braces, aligners, headgear, and other appliances to straighten teeth.

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is a dental procedure that is performed to repair or save a damaged or infected tooth. The treatment involves removing the damaged or infected tissue (pulp) inside the tooth, cleaning and disinfecting the root canals, and filling them with a special material to prevent further infection or damage.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is a cosmetic dental procedure that involves the use of various techniques to lighten the shade of natural teeth, resulting in a brighter and more attractive smile.

Gum-Treatment

Gum treatment refers to the procedures and interventions used to address and treat various gum-related issues, such as gingivitis, periodontitis, and gum recession.
