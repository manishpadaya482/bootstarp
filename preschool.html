About us

Satellite School for Children is an independent pre-school, run by Swastik Trust. The school is run in the premises in which Udgam School for Children’s Pre-Primary section was housed for a period of 7 years. The Satellite School is established along the same lines of Udgam School’s principles and ideologies. As a transitional measure, Mr. Manan Choksi, Executive Director of Udgam School has graciously agreed to be a honorary advisor of the school. Backed by strong support and expertise of experienced educators and staff, Satellite School for Children caters to individual needs and interests of each student.


Preschool Goal

Our primary goal is to pave the path for our students, so that they are completely prepared to continue their life-long learning journeys. Our unique curriculum is tailored in a way that it nurtures holistic development in a child.


Preschool Teachers and Staff

Our teachers and staff are adequately qualified, knowledgeable and suitable for providing the tiny tots a comfortable and warm environment. The teachers are trained periodically with the latest inventions and technology in early education.


Preschool Curriculum

Students are introduced to English and Hindi languages, along with maths, computers, practical life exercises, music, dance and art activities. Beyond academic goals, we see to it that the students of Satellite School for Children, are confident, compassionate and responsible. At our school, the students enjoy a plethora of activities involving unique resources that may not be commonly available elsewhere. Their learning experiences are often mingled with play to help them retain every new information received at school. We believe in innovation to achieve excellence in preschool education and that is why, we continually upgrade ourselves and introduce new methodologies to keep pace with the changing world.

Preschool Mission

SSFC adopts an amalgamation of ideologies and methodologies of the leading educationists of the world, with the help of which, we have been able to develop one of the best early childhood education curriculum. Our objective is to ensure that all the SSFC students are self-motivated, socially adaptable and develop a positive outlook towards life.

Infrastructure

Building and Infrastructure – Safe and Secure

Our school building is designed specifically to provide optimum comfort to our students. Each classroom is centrally air-conditioned with soundproof doors/windows and has an acoustic false ceiling as well as LED lights to deliver a good learning experience. Also, the lessons are made fun and enriching with smartboard technology.

The building has an elevator and broad staircases on two sides that helps evacuate the building quickly and aid the easy movement of our tiny tots. The flooring at the premises has features of reduction of noise and anti-microbial properties. We have an indoor playing area on each floor which is the wide corridor, and it helps children have ample playing space just outside their classroom. The child-friendly anti-slippery carpeting in the school building further ensures that our students don’t get hurt while playing or running.

We have a dedicated team that meticulously plans nutritious meals for our students. RO drinking water facility is housed on the floors at the preschool.

The premises of Satellite School for Children is clean, hygienic with waterless urinals and has fire safety equipment well installed and is flame resistant.

FEE REFUND POLICY FOR PRE-PRIMARY SCHOOL
  1. Admission is withdrawn before one month of start of academic year :
    •    Tuition fee for Q1 will be refunded
    •    Admission fees will be refunded. (For New admissions only)

  2. Admission is withdrawn less than one month before the start of academic year :
    •    Tuition fee for Q1 will not be refunded.

  3. Admission is withdrawn after the start of academic year :
    •    Tuition fee till the end of that quarter is to be paid. If the remaining quarters are paid, it will be refunded.

    •    Security Deposit, if paid, will be refunded.

  4. Other Terms
    •    Any fees paid for a student is not adjustable against the fee of any other student.
    •    All unpaid fees must be paid before TC/ LC is issued to the student.
    •    Refund will be processed within 3 months after the withdrawal form is submitted along with valid bank details of the parent & their ID proof.
    •    Security deposit, if any, shall be refunded without interest, after adjustment of dues, if any, on withdrawal from the school.
